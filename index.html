10 Usability Heuristics for UI design

The menu below shows Jakob Nielsen's 10 usability heiristics while attempting to employ guidelines on menu design e.g.

  1. Menu items should be precise and minimalist to optiize for usage of screen space
  2. Menus items should be clear and farmiliar to the user
  3. Menu items should be big enough to be selected individually
  4. There should be an indication of the currently selected/pointed menu item

How to use: Hover over any heuristic to temporarily enlarge it and see details. Click to keep it enlarged. Click anywhere outside to deselect.
